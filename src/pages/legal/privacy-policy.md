# Privacy Policy

We use anonymous metrics to understand how you use the Serenity/JS website and where our traffic comes from. 
We use this insight to improve our website and create a better experience for visitors. 
We do not track you, serve personalised advertising, or sell the data to anyone.

Our website is powered by [Simple Analytics](https://www.simpleanalytics.com/),
a privacy-friendly web analytics provider.
You can learn more about Simple Analytics on its website.
